Services

Architecture for the Long Now.

We believe that a home is a supportive framework for life. Selesko Studio is grounded in the discipline of resilience. We design high-performance structures that respond to their specific climate with grace, using Passive House principles to ensure that comfort and efficiency hold up against the passage of time. Whether in a dense city or a remote landscape, we design homes that don't just endure the environment but embrace it, honoring the life held within and the beauty that surrounds.

01. Feasibility & Pre-Design

The "Go/No-Go" Study.

Before you commit to a full project, we analyze your site's potential. We review zoning codes, solar access, topography, and budget constraints to determine exactly what can be built.

  • Deliverables: Zoning Summary, Massing Diagrams, Solar Study.
  • Timeline: 2 Weeks

02. Concept Design Package

Visualizing the Possible.

This is for those who need to see the vision before committing to construction. We develop the floor plans, exterior form, and key interior moments. This package is perfect for securing investors, visualizing a remodel, or planning a future build.

  • Deliverables: 3D Renderings, Presentation Floor Plans, Material Palette.
  • Timeline: 4–6 Weeks

03. Full Service

From Sketch to Keys.

Comprehensive architectural services for custom homes and complex retrofits. We manage the entire process, coordinating engineers, navigating the permitting process, and overseeing construction administration to ensure the details are built right.

  • Deliverables: Permit Set, Construction Documents, On-Site Observation.
  • Timeline: 12+ Months
