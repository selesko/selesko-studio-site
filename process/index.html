Process

We believe a successful project is built on clear communication and predictable steps. We use advanced digital tools to keep you informed, from the first sketch to the final punch list.

The Three Paths

1. The Collection — Select from our limited-run series of Selesko Studio designs. These are fully resolved concepts ready for fabrication.

2. The Custom Commission (Bespoke) — We design a unique piece specifically for your space and needs, following the full 4-step process below.

For a Building

01. Discovery & Feasibility

Defining the Constraints.

Before we draw, we listen. We analyze the site's specific microclimate (sun, wind, soil) and audit the zoning code to understand exactly what is possible. We establish a realistic budget and program early, ensuring we aren't designing a dream that cannot be built.

  • Key Milestone: The Feasibility Report

02. Concept & Simulation

Iterative Design.

This is where the narrative takes shape. We use 3D modeling and visualization from day one, allowing you to walk through the space virtually before it exists. Simultaneously, we run early energy models to ensure the form is optimized for passive heating and cooling.

  • Key Milestone: The Design Presentation Deck

03. Virtual Construction

Building it Twice.

We don't just draw plans; we build a comprehensive Building Information Model (BIM). We resolve conflicts digitally, figuring out exactly how the mechanical systems fit within the floor structure, so we don't have to solve them in the rain on the job site. This phase produces the "Instruction Manual" for your builder.

  • Key Milestone: Permit & Construction Sets

04. Stewardship (Construction Administration)

Protecting the Vision.

A drawing is only as good as its execution. During construction, Selesko Studio acts as your advocate. We answer builder questions (RFIs), review material submittals, and visit the site at critical junctures to ensure the high-performance details are executed correctly.

  • Key Milestone: Certificate of Occupancy

For an Object

01. Discovery & Context

Defining the Ritual.

An object does not exist in a vacuum; it exists in a specific moment of your day. Before we sketch, we analyze the "site"—whether it's the corner of a room that needs light or the ergonomic requirements of a dining chair. We don't just design a lamp; we design the quality of light it casts.

Key Milestone: The Object Brief & Material Palette

02. Form & Study

Iterative Modeling.

Just like our architecture, our objects are born in 3D. We explore proportion, scale, and silhouette digitally, placing the object into a virtual model of your room to test how it holds space. We create "Digital Mockups" to verify that the design feels inevitable, not just decorative.

Key Milestone: The Concept Render and/or Dimensioned Study

03. The Tectonic Detail

Engineering the Joint.

Beauty is in the assembly. How does the white oak meet the blackened steel? How is the stone anchored without visible fasteners? We produce precise shop drawings mapping every tenon, bolt, and weld—ensuring the piece is durable, honest, and built to last generations.

Key Milestone: The Fabrication / Shop Drawings

04. Fabrication & Patina

The Art of Assembly.

We transition from digital file to physical artifact. Whether we are hand-finishing the piece in our own workshop or collaborating with master local fabricators, we obsess over the final touch—the grain direction, the warmth of the LED, and the oil finish that will age gracefully.

Key Milestone: Delivery & Installation
